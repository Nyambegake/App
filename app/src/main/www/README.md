# 📱 Academic Portfolio App

A sleek, mobile-first academic portfolio application. This app allows researchers and students to carry their professional profile, publications, and research interests right in their pocket.



## ✨ Features

* **Mobile-Optimized:** Fully responsive layout designed for Android and iOS screens.
* **Offline Access:** View downloaded CVs and cached publication details on the go.
* **Interactive Timeline:** A smooth, scrollable view of your academic history.
* **Direct Contact:** Integrated buttons for email, LinkedIn, and Google Scholar.
* **Dark Mode Support:** Easy on the eyes for late-night networking.
